Track AIPAC

Track AIPAC is an initiative that is dedicated to revealing and challenging the influence of the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC) and the broader Israel lobby in U.S. democracy. By documenting financial contributions from pro-Israel entities to federal officials, Track AIPACâ€™s goal is to bring transparency and hold politicians accountable for their affiliations. Additionally, Track AIPAC calls for AIPAC and similar foreign lobbying groups to register under the Foreign Agents Registration Act (FARA) and support efforts to overturn the Citizens United decision, seeking to diminish the impact of money in politics. Their work is supported by Citizens Against AIPAC Corruption and utilizes data from sources like OpenSecrets.org and the Federal Election Commission to inform and educate the public in order to promote democratic integrity.
